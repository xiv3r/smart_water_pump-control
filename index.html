🚀 Quick Setup

  1. WiFi Setup: Connect to hotspot Auto-Pump-Config (Password: 12345678).
  2. Configure: Open 192.168.4.1 and enter your Home WiFi details.
  3. Access: After reboot, visit the device IP shown on the LCD to open the dashboard.
  4. Settings: Set Tank Height, Voltage Limits, and Auto-Retry logic in the settings menu.

🛡️ Safety & Features

  • Dual-Mode: Switch between Water Pump and Air Compressor operations.
  • Solenoid Venting: Automatic pressure release valve for compressor reliability.
  • Voltage Guard: Automatic cut-off (>250 V or <170 V).
  • Dry-Run System: Smart flow detection with Auto-Retry or Lock modes.
  • Cool-Down Logic: Hardware overheating protection for high-demand loads.
  • Smart Scheduling: DND mode for automated overnight silence.
